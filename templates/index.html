Where are all the dogs?

Welcome to our project page! This analysis takes New York City dog licensing data from the years 2013-2016 and analyzes trends in dog names, dog locations, and dog breed. In addition, use the visualization below to see the relationship between income and dog ownership. To see further analysis, use the dropdown menu to select a visualization.
