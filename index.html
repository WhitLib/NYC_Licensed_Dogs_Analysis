Welcome to our project page! This analysis takes NYC dog license data from the years 2013?-2019 and analyzes trends in dog names, dog locations, and dog breed. In addition, we attempt to see if there is a correlation between income and dog ownership, and whether dog ownership is increasing or decreasing. Take a look around at the visualizations.
